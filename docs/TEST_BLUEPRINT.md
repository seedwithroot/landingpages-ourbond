## Current Observations

### Navigation & Information Architecture

#### ❌ Homepage - Limited Internal Navigation
**Issue:** Primary landing page lacks links to explore more information on the website. All CTAs drive directly to app download/signup.

**Impact:**
- Users can't learn more about features, pricing, or use cases without committing to app download
- Reduces discovery for users in research phase
- May increase bounce rate for top-of-funnel traffic

**Measurement Opportunities:**
- Track bounce rate on homepage vs. pages with internal navigation
- Test variant with "Learn More" or "See Features" links
- Measure app download rate: immediate CTA vs. informed visitors

**Potential Solutions:**
- Add secondary navigation: "How it Works", "Features", "Pricing"
- Include in-page anchor links to feature sections
- Add footer navigation with service pages
