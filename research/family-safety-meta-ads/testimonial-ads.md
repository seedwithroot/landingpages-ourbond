## Design Notes

- Use real photos when possible (with permission)
- Stock photos should feel authentic, not overly staged
- 5-star rating prominently displayed
- Facebook-style testimonial card layout
- Purple brand color (#6B5B95) as background or accent
- Bond logo in bottom right
- Quote should be easy to read at mobile size
- Attribution should feel real (first name + last initial, or role description)
