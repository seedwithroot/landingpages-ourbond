## Campaign Overview

This folder contains research, copy, and assets for Meta advertising campaigns targeting families concerned about safety.

## Ad Formats

- **Video Ads** - including the Bond anthem video
- **Single Image Ads** - static image with copy
- **Carousel Ads** - multi-image storytelling

## Target Audience

- Parents and guardians
- Families concerned about safety
- People looking for peace of mind and emergency response

## Key Messaging Themes

Based on reviews and use cases:
- Family emergency response
- Peace of mind for loved ones
- 24/7 professional security agents
- Easy-to-use panic button
- GPS tracking for family members
