# Meta Ads Targeting - Family Safety Campaign

## Funnel Strategy Overview

This campaign uses a two-tier funnel approach targeting parents across different life stages at the top of funnel, with remarketing to engaged audiences mid-funnel.

## Top of Funnel (TOFU) - Cold Audiences

### Age Group: 45-55
1. **Parents With Adult Children 45-55**
   - Adult children (18-25+) living independently
   - Concerns: College safety, young adults living alone, travel
   - Message angle: "Keep your adult kids safe even when they're away"

2. **Parents With Teenagers 45-55**
   - Teenagers (13-17) starting to drive, date, go out
   - Concerns: Teen driving, late nights out, first jobs
   - Message angle: "Peace of mind when your teens are out"

3. **Parents With Children 45-55**
   - Younger children (under 13)
   - Concerns: School safety, family emergencies, general protection
   - Message angle: "Protect your whole family, anytime"

### Age Group: 55-65+
4. **Parents With Adult Children 55-65+**
   - Adult children likely established with own families
   - Concerns: Aging in place, medical emergencies, grandchildren
   - Message angle: "Safety for you and peace of mind for your family"

5. **Parents With Teenagers 55-65+**
   - Later-in-life parents or blended families
   - Concerns: Similar to 45-55 teen parents
   - Message angle: "Never worry when they're out late"

6. **Parents With Children 55-65+**
   - Grandparents raising grandchildren or later-in-life parents
   - Concerns: Medical emergencies, energy levels, family safety
   - Message angle: "Professional help when you need it most"

## Mid Funnel (MOFU) - Remarketing Audiences

### 7. Remarketing for Lone Workers
- **Source**: Website visitors, video viewers, engaged users interested in lone worker content
- **Persona**: Solo professionals, gig workers, remote workers
- **Message angle**: "You've seen how Bond protects lone workers—protect your family too"
- **Goal**: Cross-sell from individual/professional use to family use

### 8. Remarketing for Families
- **Source**: Family landing page visitors, family-focused content viewers, cart abandoners
- **Persona**: Already interested in Bond for family safety
- **Message angle**: "Ready to protect your family? Start your free trial"
- **Goal**: Conversion of warm leads

## Campaign Notes

### Budget Allocation Recommendation
- **70% TOFU**: Cast wide net to find new customers
- **30% MOFU**: Convert warm engaged audiences

### Creative Testing Strategy
- Test different message angles per age group
- Separate ad sets for each audience segment
- Track performance by parent type to optimize spend

### Key Metrics by Funnel Stage
**TOFU:**
- Video view rate
- Cost per video view
- Landing page visitors
- Cost per landing page view

**MOFU:**
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Cost per click (CPC)
- Conversion rate
- Cost per acquisition (CPA)

## Exclusions
- Exclude converters from TOFU campaigns
- Exclude MOFU audiences from TOFU campaigns to avoid overlap
- Consider excluding lone worker converts from family remarketing (unless cross-sell strategy)
