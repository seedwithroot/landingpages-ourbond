## Design Notes

**Visual Style:**  
- Clean, cinematic, high-contrast  
- Dark backgrounds (pure black or dark urban) to make text pop  
- Professional photography look, not stock photo feel  
- Keep faces visible and relatable  
- Minimal brand elements - let the testimonial be the hero

**Text Overlay Treatment:**  
- Quote in large, readable sans-serif font  
- Attribution line below in smaller weight  
- Consider quotation marks as design element  
- White text with subtle drop shadow for legibility  
- Leave breathing room - don't crowd the composition

**Bond Branding:**  
- Small Bond logo in corner (consistent placement)  
- Don't compete with quote for attention  
- Brand appears in ad copy, doesn't need to dominate image

## Testing Strategy

**Test 1: Authority vs. Customer**  
- Pit Ad 2A (Raymond Kelly - authority) vs. Ad 3A (Jennifer J. - customer)  
- Same audience, same budget split  
- Which proof type drives more clicks from warm audiences?

**Test 2: Quote Length**  
- Full quote vs. pull quote  
- Does shorter, punchier text perform better?  
- Test on Jennifer J. ad first (she has shorter quote)

**Test 3: Visual Tone**  
- Confident/empowered vs. Warm/reassured  
- Jennifer J. walking confidently vs. close intimate portrait  
- Which emotional tone resonates for personal safety message?

---

## Audience Targeting

**Primary:** Remarketing audiences  
- Video viewers (25%+)  
- Carousel engagers  
- Landing page visitors, no conversion  
- Add to cart, no purchase

**Secondary:** Warm audiences  
- Engaged with Instagram/Facebook content  
- Lookalike audiences (1-2%)  

**Exclude:** Recent converters (last 30 days)

---

## Performance Benchmarks (Expected)

For remarketing single image ads:  
- CTR: 1.5-3% (warm audiences should beat cold by 2-3x)  
- CPC: $0.80-$2.50 depending on market  
- Engagement rate: 4-8%  

If any ad drops below 1% CTR after 1000 impressions to warm audience → pause and test new visual or quote edit.
