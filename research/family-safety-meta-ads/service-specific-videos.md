## Targeting Recommendations by Service

### Track Me On The Go
- Best for: Parents with teen drivers, parents with commuters
- Age groups: All (45-55, 55-65+)

### Ready An Agent
- Best for: Parents with college students, adult children living alone
- Age groups: 45-55 (adult children), 55-65+ (adult children)

### Video Monitor Me
- Best for: Parents concerned about specific threats, home alone situations
- Age groups: All, especially 55-65+

### Send Me A Car
- Best for: Parents with teens/young adults, social situations
- Age groups: 45-55 (teenagers, adult children)

### Siren
- Best for: Parents with kids in urban areas, personal safety concerns
- Age groups: All

### Dial 911
- Best for: Medical emergencies, elderly parents, all-around safety
- Age groups: 55-65+

### Road Assistance
- Best for: Parents with drivers, commuters, families
- Age groups: All

### Security Check
- Best for: Elderly parents, medical conditions, kids home alone
- Age groups: 55-65+ (for elderly parents), 45-55 (for younger kids)
