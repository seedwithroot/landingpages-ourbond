# Bond (OurBond.com) — Top-of-Funnel Ad Package

**Brand:** Bond by TG-17, Inc.  
**Site:** https://www.ourbond.com  
**Core Product:** AI-powered personal security app with 24/7 live Security Agents  
**CTA Priority:** Download app (3-day free trial) or Schedule a Briefing (B2B)

---

## CAMPAIGN STRATEGY OVERVIEW

### Who We're Targeting (Audiences)

| Segment | Intent Signal | Channel Priority |
|---|---|---|
| **Women, 18–45, urban** | Walk home safe, personal safety app | Google Search, Meta, TikTok |
| **Young professionals, commuters** | Safety app, security on the go | Google Search, Meta |
| **Parents / Families** | Track family, family safety app | Meta, Google |
| **High-net-worth individuals** | Executive protection, personal security | Google Search, LinkedIn |
| **HR Directors / Enterprises** | Employee safety benefit, workforce security | LinkedIn, Google |
| **University Administrators** | Campus safety, student security | Google Display, LinkedIn |

### Funnel Stage: Awareness → Consideration

At the top of funnel, the goal is to:
1. **Introduce** the Bond brand to people who don't know it exists yet
2. **Create urgency** around a felt problem (feeling unsafe, traveling alone, walking home at night)
3. **Drive app downloads** (B2C) or **Schedule a Briefing** (B2B)
